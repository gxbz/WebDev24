What is Astronomy?
Astronomy is the study of everything in the universe beyond Earth's atmosphere. That includes objects we can see with our naked eyes, like the Sun, the Moon, the planets, and the stars. It also includes objects we can only see indirectly, like comets, galaxies, and black holes. Astronomy also studies phenomena that originate outside of the atmosphere, like cosmic background radiation.
